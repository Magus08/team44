About Us

Team 44 is a residential soccer team
aimed at serving the youth in and around
Ewutu Senya East District. The team
scouts and helps with player transfers to
European countries (Belgium, Germany,
Saudi-Arabia) and also African countries
(Egypt, Ethiopia and South-Africa)
